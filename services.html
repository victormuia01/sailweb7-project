Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical website project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but a standard business website typically takes 4-6 weeks from start to finish. More complex projects like e-commerce sites or custom web applications may take 8-12 weeks.

Do you provide website hosting?

Yes, we offer reliable hosting solutions as part of our web maintenance packages. We can also help you set up hosting with a provider of your choice if you prefer.

Can you work with our existing design or website?

Absolutely! We can redesign and rebuild your existing website or work with your current design to improve functionality and performance.

What technologies do you use?

We use a variety of modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Node.js, PHP, and various CMS platforms like WordPress, depending on your project requirements.

Do you offer training after the website is launched?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training to ensure you're comfortable managing your website's content. We also create detailed documentation for future reference.
