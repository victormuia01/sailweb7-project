Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. All payments are processed securely. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger projects.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for larger projects. Typically, we require a 50% deposit to start the project, with the balance due upon completion. Contact us to discuss customized payment options.

What's included in the support period?

During the support period, we provide bug fixes, minor updates, and technical assistance. Major changes or new features would be quoted separately. We're here to ensure your website runs smoothly after launch.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time. We'll apply a pro-rated credit for any payments you've already made towards your current plan.

Do you offer custom pricing for specific needs?

Yes, we understand that every business has unique needs. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your specific requirements. We'll work with you to create a solution that fits your budget and goals.

Are there any hidden costs?

No, our pricing is completely transparent. The prices shown include VAT and cover all the features listed for each plan. The only additional costs would be for custom features not included in the selected package.
